Send your opponent's cards from their hand, deck, field, and drop to your Prison to power up your units with Seraph Purelight! Use "Blitz Staff, Muna" to draw cards whenever your opponent calls a card from Prison. "Security Upgrader" allows you to find Seraph Purelight more easily, and "Aurora Battle Princess, Corner Citrus" gives you more attacks. The deck's attack becomes extremely lethal once you have 10 cards in your Prison, which causes Purelight to give your front row to gain 25k power and +1 critical!
