Use Varga's skil to retire the front row rear-guards of both fighters, while conserving your card advantage with cards like Shuura and Boulder Axe! Use your vanguards' Divine Skill to further pressure your opponent and gain extra cards by attacking with Twin Drive. Once you ride "Rakshasa" and unleash Malwyrm, you can retire the back row of both fighters, and you'l be able to activate your Divine Skill regardless of your opponent's damage!
