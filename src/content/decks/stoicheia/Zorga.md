Bind orders from your drop zone to gain access to them for Alchemagic, the ability to combine two normal orders! Your units can revive themselves with Zorga or their own skills, and can convert themselves into more card advantage. Use your order cards to increase your power, and pressure your opponent with Divine Skill by restanding your rear-guards with critical!
