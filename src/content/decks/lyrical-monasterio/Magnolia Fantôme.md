Use Crimson Moon orders and "MateS With U!!" to swarm your opponent with units, and then give them the ability to attack from the back row with Magnolia's skill! Magnolia becomes a grade 4 with 3 drive checks with your opponent's vanguard is grade 3 or greater, making their attacks even more threatening! Use Magnolia's Fantome Skill to return your units to your hand, make give your LettiaMateS units more shield!
