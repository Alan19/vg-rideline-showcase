Use Kairi's skills to get extra attacks and return advantage-generating rear-guards to your hand, and then calling them again! Your rear-guards can give you a 5th attack before you persona ride, and when you persona ride, you get drive +1! Increase your hand size even further by return your rear-guards back to your hand after they attack, increasing your defenses even more!
